ABOUT US

Thiru. N. Natarajan, Senior Advocate (1931 – 2021)
A Legacy Etched in Law

Thiru. N. Natarajan was one of the most formidable minds in Indian criminal law, whose legal career spanned over six remarkable decades. Beginning in 1955 under the tutelage of stalwarts like P.S. Kailasam and S. Mohan Kumaramangalam, he quickly distinguished himself through his analytical brilliance, compelling courtroom presence, and unwavering dedication to the cause of justice.

Appointed as Public Prosecutor in 1975 and designated as a Senior Advocate in 1978, he became a household name in the legal fraternity. From prosecuting in the historic Bofors case and the Bombay Bomb Blasts trial to defending convicts in the Rajiv Gandhi assassination matter before the Hon’ble Supreme Court, Thiru. N. Natarajan’s career reflected a rare balance of prosecutorial rigor and deep constitutional compassion.

Admired for his humility, clarity of thought and impeccable ethics, he mentored generations of lawyers and judges, many of whom today occupy leading positions in the judiciary and the Bar. His legacy endures—not just in law reports and landmark judgements, but in the values he lived by and instilled in others: fairness, mentorship and intellectual integrity.

The N. Natarajan Charitable Trust
Advancing Legal Excellence & Empowerment

Established by the juniors and disciples of Late Thiru. N. Natarajan, the N. Natarajan Charitable Trust stands as a living tribute to a life devoted to law, learning and service. With a mission to continue his vision of accessible and ethical legal education, the Trust focuses on empowering the next generation of students and lawyers—particularly those from underrepresented backgrounds.

Now in its decennial year, the Trust has been at the forefront of initiatives that blend scholarship with service: conducting National and State-level Moot Court Competitions, organizing Law Lecture Series featuring leading legal minds and sponsoring the education of deserving students who exemplify promise and perseverance.

Its core objectives are rooted in creating pathways for academic excellence, expanding access to legal education and nurturing a generation of legal professionals driven by principle, empathy, and public spirit.

The Moot Court Competition 2025
Honouring Legacy, Inspiring Advocacy

The N. Natarajan Charitable Trust Moot Court Competition, hosted in collaboration with the Madras Bar Association, is one of Tamil Nadu’s most prestigious State-level moot events. It is open to law students across Tamil Nadu and Puducherry and is envisioned as more than a contest—it is a courtroom experience.

What sets this competition apart is the depth of its authenticity. Adjudicated by sitting judges of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India and Hon’ble High Court of Madras and leading Senior Advocates of the Madras Bar, every round mirrors the decorum, intensity and expectations of real-world legal proceedings.

Celebrating the Trusts decennium, the competition pays homage to Thiru. N. Natarajan’s legacy—championing the art of advocacy, the discipline of research and the ethics of legal practice. It is a platform where young minds not only test their skills but also encounter the ideals that defined Thiru. Natarajan’s life and practice.
